About Me

Welcome to Assad's Carrom Boards! I'm Assad, a passionate craftsman dedicated to creating high-quality carrom boards that bring families and friends together.

With years of experience in woodworking and a deep love for the traditional game of carrom, I specialize in handcrafting premium boards using the finest materials. Each board is meticulously designed to meet professional standards while maintaining the authentic feel that makes carrom such a beloved game.

What I Offer:

  • Custom handcrafted carrom boards
  • Professional tournament-grade boards
  • Board restoration and repair services
  • Premium accessories and playing pieces
  • Personalized designs and engravings

Whether you're a casual player looking for family fun or a serious competitor seeking tournament-quality equipment, I'm here to craft the perfect carrom board for your needs. Every piece is made with attention to detail and a commitment to excellence.

My Journey:

My passion for carrom began in childhood, playing with family and friends. Over the years, I noticed the importance of having a quality board that enhances the playing experience. This led me to start crafting my own boards, combining traditional techniques with modern precision.

Today, I'm proud to serve carrom enthusiasts across the region, providing boards that not only meet competitive standards but also become cherished centerpieces for family gatherings and tournaments alike.
